      • Introduciton to the electronics and optical properties of solids. Emphasis is on concepts and models of phonons and electronics energy bands and applied to metals, semiconductors, and insulators. Elementary quantum and statistical mechanics concepts are utilized.

        3. LD - laser diode
        4. Photo detector

        1. MOS capacitor
        2. MOS transistor
        3. Thin film transistor for LCD
